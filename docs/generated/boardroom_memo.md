# Prioritization Boardroom — Decision Memo

## Roadmap considered
1. App Performance and Stability Issues — reach 32, impact 2.56/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 34.8
2. UI/UX & Mobile Functionality Hurdles — reach 27, impact 1.89/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 21.2
3. Functionality & Usability Issues — reach 15, impact 2.87/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 19.4
4. Technical Performance & Stability — reach 15, impact 2.53/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 16.9
5. Intrusive AI Features — reach 11, impact 3.0/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 14.0
6. False Offline Status — reach 10, impact 2.9/4, effort 2.0w, RICE 12.8

# Panel debate
## Growth PM
As a Growth PM, my top priority is to maximize reach and reduce churn, which is why I strongly advocate for tackling App Performance and Stability Issues first. With a RICE score of 34.8, this opportunity has the highest potential impact on our user base, affecting 32% of our users. I'd also consider prioritizing UI/UX & Mobile Functionality Hurdles second, as it still reaches a significant portion of our users (27%) and has a notable impact. In contrast, I'd deprioritize Intrusive AI Features, despite its high impact score, as it only reaches 11% of our users, which is relatively low compared to the other opportunities. By addressing the top two issues, we can significantly improve the overall user experience, reduce churn, and set a strong foundation for future growth.

## Engineering Lead
As the Engineering Lead, I strongly believe we should prioritize "App Performance and Stability Issues" first, given its high RICE score of 34.8 and significant impact on our users. This issue has the potential to greatly improve the overall reliability of our app, reducing instability and frustration for our users. I would also consider prioritizing "Functionality & Usability Issues" second, as it has a high impact score and addresses key usability concerns. In contrast, I would deprioritize "Intrusive AI Features" for now, as its high effort and lower RICE score make it a less feasible and less urgent fix, and instead focus on stabilizing our core app experience before introducing new features.

## Finance (CFO)
As the CFO, I strongly believe we should prioritize "App Performance and Stability Issues" first, given its high RICE score of 34.8 and significant impact on our users. This initiative has the potential to drive substantial revenue and retention benefits, and its relatively high reach and impact scores justify the 2.0 weeks of effort required. I'd also consider prioritizing "Functionality & Usability Issues" second, as its high impact score of 2.87 suggests it could have a significant effect on user satisfaction. In contrast, I'd deprioritize "Intrusive AI Features" despite its high impact score, as its relatively low reach and RICE score suggest a more niche benefit that may not justify the significant effort required, and I'm skeptical of its potential ROI.

---
## Decision
The top 3 priorities, in order, are: 
1. App Performance and Stability Issues - I'm prioritizing this first because it has the highest RICE score and affects the largest portion of our user base, making it a crucial step in improving overall user experience.
2. Functionality & Usability Issues - This comes second as it has a high impact score, indicating a significant effect on user satisfaction, and addressing it will help stabilize our core app experience.
3. UI/UX & Mobile Functionality Hurdles - I'm prioritizing this third because, although it has a significant reach, its impact score is lower than the top two, but still important for enhancing the mobile user experience.

## Dissent
The leaders disagreed on the prioritization of "Intrusive AI Features", with the Growth PM, Engineering Lead, and CFO all deprioritizing it due to its low reach and RICE score despite its high impact score. I'm knowingly setting aside the Growth PM's concern that this feature could have a high impact on user experience, and I'm comfortable carrying this risk because the data suggests that only 11% of our users are affected, which is a relatively niche benefit that doesn't justify the significant effort required.

## Next step
I will assign two engineers to tackle the App Performance and Stability Issues this sprint, and I'll pull in the design team to work on the UI/UX aspects of the Functionality & Usability Issues. I expect to see a detailed plan of action from the engineers within the first week, and a prototype of the redesigned functionality from the design team by the end of the sprint. I'll also schedule a meeting with the Growth PM and Engineering Lead to review our progress and adjust our priorities as needed, ensuring we're on track to deliver a more stable and user-friendly app experience.
